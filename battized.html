BATTIZED

Branding . Print Design . Vector Graphics

Modeled after a 35 ST Contender, the Battized branding and visuals use a clean aesthetic and minimal design to embody the modern and innovative feel of this performance fishing boat.

PRINT WORK




Performance shirts printed using sublimated dye on white shirts for a colored version of the boat and screen printing for the boat outline. Design was also reproduced on cotton shirts, coolers, and embroidered.
